Objective

To apply advanced Python techniques for data transformation, time-series analysis, and feature engineering using historical stock market data.

Dataset

Use the Apple Inc. (AAPL) Historical Stock Market Dataset from Yahoo Finance.

Dataset Link:
https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/AAPL/history/

Steps to Download:

1. Open the dataset link.
2. Click on Historical Data.
3. Select 5 years date range.
4. Click Download to download the dataset as a CSV file.

Dataset Features

The dataset contains:

- Date
- Open Price
- High Price
- Low Price
- Close Price
- Adjusted Close Price
- Trading Volume

Create meaningful features such as:

- Daily Price Change

- Any additional features that improve analysis
