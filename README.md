# Apple (AAPL) Historical Stock Analysis Using Python

## Project Overview

This project explores Apple's (AAPL) historical stock market performance using Python. The analysis covers five years of stock data (2021–2026) and focuses on data transformation, time-series analysis, feature engineering, and data visualization.

The goal is to identify market trends, analyze investor activity, and generate insights from historical stock price movements.

---

## Dataset

Source: Yahoo Finance

Dataset: Apple Inc. (AAPL) Historical Stock Data

Features:
- Date
- Open Price
- High Price
- Low Price
- Close Price
- Volume

Period Covered:
- June 2021 – June 2026

## Key Insights

### Closing Price Trend
Apple's stock maintained a strong long-term upward trend despite temporary market corrections.

### Trading Volume Trend
Trading activity fluctuated throughout the period, while stock prices continued to rise, indicating strong investor confidence.

### Moving Average Analysis
The 7-Day and 30-Day Moving Averages confirmed a sustained bullish trend with temporary periods of weakness followed by recovery.

### Daily Return Analysis
Daily returns fluctuated around zero, showing a balanced pattern of gains and losses with generally stable performance.

### Volatility Analysis
Volatility remained moderate for most of the period, with temporary spikes indicating periods of increased market uncertainty.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that Apple experienced strong long-term growth between 2021 and 2026. Despite fluctuations in trading activity, daily returns, and volatility, the stock maintained an overall bullish trend, reflecting sustained investor confidence and market resilience.
